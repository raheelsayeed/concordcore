Understanding Cholesterol

Elevated cholesterol (a fat-like substance that comes from animal foods or is made in your body) can clog arteries that reduce blood flow to the organs and may lead to heart attach, stroke or other cardiovascular event.

  • HDL = Good
    High density lipoprotein is known as good cholesterol

  • LDL = Bad
    Low density lipoprotein is known as bad cholesterol

  • Triglyceride
    is the most common type of fat in the body

  • Total Cholesterol
    is equal to your HDL + LDL + 1/5th Triglyceride level
