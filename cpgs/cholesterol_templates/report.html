Understanding Cholesterol

Cholesterol is a fat-like substance that comes from animal foods or the body and if elevated, can clog arteries and lead to heart attack or stroke.

  • HDL = Good: High density lipoprotein is known as good cholesterol
  • LDL = Bad: Low density lipoprotein is known as good cholesterol
  • Triglyceride is the most common type of fat in the body
  • Total Cholesterol: HDL + LDL + 1/5th Triglyceride level

Tracking your cholesterol levels over time and taking steps to reduce high cholesterol can help heart health.
